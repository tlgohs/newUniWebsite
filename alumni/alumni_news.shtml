Former Uni High Teacher Audrey Wells (taught English and founded the Film Study class at Uni from 1983 to 2001) has co-authored, along with Perry Morris and Joseph Muskin the book:The Art Theatre: Playing Movies for 100 Years.The book celebrates the Art Theatre in downtown Champaign IL that celebrated its centennial November 12, 2013

Former Uni principal and math professor Dr. Robert Davis brought and led the Madison Project, an innovative curriculum for teaching mathematics, to Webster in the 1960's. This website provides access to digitized videos created from the original inservice films produced by the Madison Project, as well as information on the curriculum.

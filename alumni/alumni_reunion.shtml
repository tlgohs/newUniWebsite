Alumni Reunions

The University High School Alumni Department is here to help you make your reunion a success. Whether you’re returning for Alumni Reunion Weekend or events in between, we're happy to help you renew friendships, rekindle memories, and reconnect you with Uni High. If you are interested in coordinating a reunion or think it is time to get a group together, please let us know.
